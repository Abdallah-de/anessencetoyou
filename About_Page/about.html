Welcome to the website dedicated to Barangay Disaster Preparedness During Typhoon and Fire

PURPOSE AND DESCRIPTION

In producing the 2D animated film “Essence” is about helping people to understand the importance of disaster preparedness when typhoon occurs in the area. The short, animated film was based on the disaster preparedness plan of the residents in Barangay Bonga Menor which intends to provide entertainment to the Filipinos, and it is also conceptualized and designed to raise public awareness and education in reducing loss of life, personal injuries, and damage from storms. This capstone project aims to address on developing strategies and initiatives to enhance the ability of communities to effectively respond to and mitigate the impact of the typhoons and fires.

OBJECTIVES

This research project seeks to create an educational animated film that will raise awareness about disaster preparedness plan of the residents of Barangay Bonga Menor during typhoon disasters in the Philippines, and to implement the typhoon and fire disaster preparedness plan of the Barangay Bonga Menor, Bustos, Bulacan.

SCOPE AND DELIMITATION

The scope of the study focuses on Barangay disaster preparedness during typhoon, and fire events in Barangay Bonga Menor. The study aims to promote the importance of disaster preparedness plan before these huge natural disasters will hit the in-danger areas throughout the 2D animated infomercial film. The animated film will only use Tagalog as its main language, and an English subtitle. This study will be exploring the effectiveness of the 2D animated film to the audiences through the utilization of questionnaires by Google forms to the viewers, the collected data will be analyzed using statistical treatment specifically descriptive statistics.
